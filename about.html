SBC UKFCET

The official approval of the petition to form the UKF College of Engineering and Technology Industry Applications Society Student Branch Chapter in the Kerala Section. The approval date is August 10, 2018 and the geo-code is SBC15125. It is being the double milestone chapter that is 400th IAS Chapter worldwide as well as the 50th IAS SB Chapter in Kerala section. Mr Athul M V is the Chapter Advisor and Ms Layaraj is the chapter chapter. IAS SBC UKFCET was officially inaugurated on September 28 by Prof Muhammed Kasim, Chair IA/IE/PELS Jt.chapter Kerala. Within a short period of time IAS SBC UKFCET had completed more than 50 events. The vibrant volunteers of IAS SBC UKFCET had showcased their talent in technical concerns as well as humanitarian issues.
